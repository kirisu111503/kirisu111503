## 🔬 About My Research
I am an independent researcher and software engineer specializing in artificial intelligence, automation, and technological advancement. Driven by a goal to make automated systems more perceptive, my current work explores how AI can transition from basic object identification to true situational awareness. 

My active research focuses on **Visual Relationship Detection (VRD)** and scene reasoning engines designed to enhance automated early-warning systems. I advocate for open science, hosting my codebases and research models publicly to foster collaboration and reproducibility.
